Day 1
Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Keto Bowl

¾ cup full-fat Greek yogurt
1 tbsp chia seeds
2 tbsp walnuts, chopped
4–5 raspberries or blackberries
Mix everything in a bowl and enjoy.

Lunch: Marinated Tofu & Veggie Salad

½ block firm tofu, cold, diced
1 cup spinach, chopped
¼ bell pepper, diced
½ cucumber, diced
1 tbsp olives, sliced
1 tbsp olive oil
Juice of ½ lemon
1 tbsp fresh basil or cilantro, chopped
Salt and pepper
Toss ingredients in a bowl, let marinate in fridge 15–30 minutes before eating.

Snack: Stuffed Avocado

1 avocado, halved and pitted
¼ cup cottage cheese or feta
Squeeze lemon juice
Pepper/chili flakes
Fill avocado with cheese, top with lemon and pepper.

Dinner: Caprese Zucchini Ribbon Salad

1 zucchini, peeled into thin ribbons
4 oz mozzarella balls, halved
½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
1 tbsp olive oil
Fresh basil
Salt, pepper
Mix all on a plate. Drizzle with oil, season, and toss with basil.

Day 2
Breakfast: Avocado, Tomato, & Feta Plate

1 ripe avocado, sliced
1 tomato, sliced
¼ cup feta, crumbled
Salt, pepper, olive oil
Basil leaves
Layer avocado and tomato, top with feta, olive oil, and basil.

Lunch: Broccoli & Halloumi Toss (cold)

1 cup raw broccoli florets, chopped small
½ block halloumi, cubed
2 tbsp sunflower seeds
1 tbsp olive oil
Juice ½ lemon
Salt, pepper
Mix all in a bowl; ready to eat.

Snack: Yogurt & Nut Bowl

½ cup full-fat Greek yogurt
1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
1 tbsp walnuts
Combine and eat.

Dinner: Mediterranean Lettuce Cups

Large romaine leaves (as “cups”)
½ cup marinated artichoke hearts, chopped
½ cup cherry tomatoes, halved
¼ cup olives, sliced
¼ cup crumbled feta
1 tbsp olive oil
Mix filling, scoop into lettuce leaves, fold and eat.

Day 3
Breakfast: Berry-Cucumber Yogurt Bowl

Lunch: Spinach & Tofu Pesto Salad

1 cup spinach

1 zucchini, cut into ribbons
1 tbsp pesto

Toss everything well in a bowl.

Snack: Celery & Whipped Feta

Celery, sliced into sticks
¼ cup whipped feta
Dip and eat.

Dinner: Caprese & Avocado Platter

1 avocado, sliced
1 tomato, sliced
¼ cup mozzarella balls, halved
Basil, olive oil, salt, pepper
Layer on a plate and drizzle with oil.
